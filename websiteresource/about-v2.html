About

A clean record of every trip.
That's the whole idea.

Boatcheckin is recordkeeping software for charter operators. We help captains document waivers, safety briefings, manifests, and audit trails — without paper clipboards and without chaos at the dock.

Start Free
What we do

Document. Organize. Retain. Prove.

Every feature in Boatcheckin maps to one of these four. If a feature doesn't strengthen the record, it doesn't ship.

Document

Capture guest identity, emergency contacts, waiver signatures, and safety-card acknowledgments — collected from the guest's own phone. No app install, no friction.

Organize

Per trip. Per vessel. Per operator. Manifests in USCG format. Everything exportable as PDF or CSV on demand, without support tickets.

Retain

Compliance-relevant records retained for the period your jurisdiction specifies. Every retention window is exportable before it closes.

Prove

Waiver records are cryptographically hashed — any modification is detectable. Every state change is logged. When the question comes two years later, the answer is already on file.

Why we built it

The answer to "where is the paperwork" should never be "somewhere on the boat."

Most charter operators we've talked to can describe, in detail, the one bad moment that changed how they think about documentation. A guest made a claim months after a trip. An insurer asked for the signed waiver. A regulator wanted the manifest from a specific date.

Charter operators don't lack diligence. They lack a place for the diligence to land — something that captures the briefing, the signature, and the head count at the moment they happen, and keeps them in a shape the next person who asks can actually read.

That's the gap Boatcheckin fills. Not insurance. Not legal advice. Not a replacement for good judgment on the water. Just a clean record, kept the way a small operation needs it kept.

How we think

Five principles we hold to on purpose.

These are the decisions we've had to make more than once, written down so we don't have to make them again.

Operator-first, always.

The captain is the operator of record. Every feature either serves the captain's judgment or gets out of the way. Guests are served because the operator chooses to serve them.

Evidence, not advice.

We record what operators direct. We don't draft waivers, interpret statutes, or tell a captain what to say at the briefing. Our job is to hold the record.

Free where it matters.

Solo captains — the tier carrying the most personal exposure — get Boatcheckin free, with no time limit. That's a structural commitment.

Built to survive two years of silence.

Records are hashed for integrity, exportable without us, legible without training, and retained for the statutory window.

Boring where it counts.

Manifests look like manifests, waivers look like waivers, and the record reads the way the next person expects it to read.

What we're not

As important as what we do.

Clarity comes from the work we've chosen not to take on.

×

Not a law firm.

Nothing on this site is legal advice. Waiver text and operational decisions remain the operator's to review with a licensed attorney.

×

Not an insurance broker.

Boatcheckin does not sell, solicit, or service insurance. Where insurance partners are referenced, they are licensed agents and carriers.

×

Not a booking platform.

We sit after the booking, not in it. The trip arrives with a confirmed guest list; what happens next is recordkeeping, not sales.

×

Not a substitute for the captain.

No software makes the go/no-go call. A clean record supports the captain's judgment — it does not replace it.

×

Not a data broker.

Guest information belongs to the operator and the guest. We don't sell it. We don't license it. We don't build lookalike audiences from it.
